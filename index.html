About the platform

Built by the department, for the department

BCA Alumni Connect is the official networking bridge between current BCA students and graduates of National College. It exists to make one thing easy: reaching out to someone who has already been where you're standing now.

Every profile is maintained by the Department of BCA. Students can look up alumni by the skills they need guidance on, the companies they're aiming for, or simply say hello.
